About Me

Hello!

My name is Ellen. I love creating beautiful and interactive things on the web. I am a motivated front-end developer with over four years of experience in web content publishing and project management.

Before I started coding I worked in communications for several years. This helped me develop strong communication and organization skills which come in handy when I dive into a new coding project. I've always wanted to do something a little more creative, and that's why I started coding.

When I'm not coding I like to draw and paint or volunteer at one of the many film festivals in Toronto.
